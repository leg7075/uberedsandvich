UBERED SANDVICH

Ubered Sandvich is a 16 year old music producer from Canada. He started producing in 2014 and has been honing his skills since. Inspired by genres from all over EDM, he strives to find his own unique sound that is distinctly his.


Lately, he has been experimenting with a fusion of breakcore drums and atmospheric sounds woven together to create something greater than the sum of its parts. Past experiments have also included things like lofi, melodic dubstep, and techno.


Ubered Sandvich continues to create electronic music using Ableton Live 11 in a journey to discover the musical styling that resonates within him the most.
